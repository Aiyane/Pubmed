Leguminivora glycinivorella


文献标题:Phenotypic Plasticity of HSP70s Gene Expression during Diapause: Signs of Evolutionary Responses to Cold Stress among Soybean Pod Borer Populations ( Leguminivora glycinivorella ) in Northeast of China

PMID:25330365

PMCID:4198119

期刊:PLoS ONE

作者： Wang Ling Yang Shuai Han Lanlan Fan Dong Zhao Kuijun *
年份： 5 9 2014


摘要

   RNAi was used to study the effect of HSP70 expression on cold-stress resistance

   Similar to conditions when HSP70 expression was inhibited, Pyrrhocoris apterus has a less intense response to cold resistance in diapause, and the pupae of Sarcophaga crassipalpis have feeble cold resistance HSP70 expression plays an important role in increasing insect resistance to cold stress

正文

   Similar to conditions when HSP70 expression was inhibited, Pyrrhocoris apterus has a less intense response to cold resistance in diapause, and the pupae of Sarcophaga crassipalpis have feeble cold resistance HSP70 expression plays an important role in increasing insect resistance to cold stress.
