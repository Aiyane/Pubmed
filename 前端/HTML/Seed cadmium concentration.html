   FRAP (ferric reducing ability of plasma) concentration, GSH/hGSH content, and GST activity showed a pronounced exposure time-dependent response.

   Results of two-way analysis of exposure time and Cd concentration on the concentration of GSH, MDA and proline, total antioxidant capacity (FRAP) and GST activity of germinating soybean seed extract.

   GST concentration germinating soybean seeds after different periods of exposure.

   Cd concentration impacted markedly MDA content and proline content as well as GST activity, while the interaction of exposure time and cadmium concentration has a profound effect on MDA and proline contents ( Table 2 ).

   The present study indicated that, challenged by Cd, MDA content and proline level of germinating soybean seeds responded in a exposure time, treatment concentration and exposure time × treatment concentration interacting manner; at Cd200 GST responded to both the exposure time and treatment concentration, while FRAP and GSH responded to exposure time ( Table 2 ).
