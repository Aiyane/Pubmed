Leaflet area


文献标题:Confirmation of the pleiotropic control of leaflet shape and number of seeds per pod by the Ln gene in induced soybean mutants.

PMID:29085246

作者：Sayama T(1)(2), Tanabata T(3), Saruta M(2)(4), Yamada T(1), Anai T(5), Kaga A(1), Ishimoto M(1).
年份：2017 Sep


摘要

   In this study, we investigated the pleiotropic effect of soybean Ln gene on leaflet shape and NSP by using two independent soybean Gm-jag1 mutants and four ln near isogenic lines (NILs).

   Furthermore, one mutant line with narrow leaflets showed significantly higher NSP than that in the wild (or Ln) genotype, indicating that soybean Ln gene pleiotropically controls leaflet shape and NSP.
