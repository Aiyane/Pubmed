PMID:16531054 [Indexed for MEDLINE]

作者：Eller MH(1), Warner AL, Knap HT.
年份：2006 Jan


摘要

   The DAO frequent representation in EST libraries constructed from root tissue and elevated steady-state levels in roots compared to above ground tissues show DAO is critical for regulation of putrescine content in soybean roots.
