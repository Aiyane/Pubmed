Soybean Dwarf Virus Infection


文献标题:Identification of a maize chlorotic dwarf virus silencing suppressor protein.

PMID:28160664 [Indexed for MEDLINE]

作者：Stewart LR(1), Jarugula S(2), Zhao Y(2), Qu F(2), Marty D(3).
年份：2017 Apr


摘要

   Maize chlorotic dwarf virus (MCDV), a member of the genus Waikavirus, family Secoviridae, has a 11784 nt (+)ssRNA genome that encodes a 389kDa proteolytically processed polyprotein.
