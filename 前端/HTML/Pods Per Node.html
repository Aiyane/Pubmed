Pods Per Node


文献标题:QTL mapping of ten agronomic traits on the soybean ( Glycine max L. Merr.) genetic map and their association with EST markers.

PMID:15067400 [Indexed for MEDLINE]

作者：Zhang WK(1), Wang YJ, Luo GZ, Zhang JS, He CY, Wu XL, Gai JY, Chen SY.
年份：2004 Apr


摘要

   EST marker GmKF059a, encoding a repressor protein and mapped on group C2, accounted for about 20% of the total variation of days to flowering, plant height, lodging and nodes on the main stem, respectively.
