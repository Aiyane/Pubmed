Seed gamma-tocopherol content


文献标题:GmTMT2a from soybean elevates the α-tocopherol content in corn and Arabidopsis.

PMID:23645501 [Indexed for MEDLINE]

作者：Zhang L(1), Luo Y, Zhu Y, Zhang L, Zhang W, Chen R, Xu M, Fan Y, Wang L.
年份：2013 Oct


摘要

   Tocopherol content was investigated in 15 soybean cultivars and GmTMT2 was isolated from five varieties based on tocopherol content.

   Overexpression of GmTMT2a enhanced α-tocopherol content 4-6-fold in transgenic Arabidopsis, and α-tocopherol content increased 3-4.

   5-fold in transgenic maize seed, which correlated with the accumulation of GmTMT2a.
