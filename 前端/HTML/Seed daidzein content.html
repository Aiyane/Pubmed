文献标题:Expression of flavonoid 6-hydroxylase candidate genes in normal and mutant soybean genotypes for glycitein content.

PMID:23695494 [Indexed for MEDLINE]

作者：Artigot MP(1), Baes M, Daydé J, Berger M.
年份：2013 Jul


摘要

   In this study, the CYP71D9 gene (F6H1) and two other candidates (F6H2 and F6H3) were studied using contrasted soybean cultivars for glycitein content (0, 35, 55 and 80 %).

   F6H1 and F6H2 were not expressed in the developing seed.

   Thus, this F6H3 gene is a good potential candidate for glycitein biosynthesis in soybean seed.

文献标题:LC/UV/ESI-MS analysis of isoflavones in Edamame and Tofu soybeans.

PMID:15137811 [Indexed for MEDLINE]

作者：Wu Q(1), Wang M, Sciarappa WJ, Simon JE.
年份：2004 May 19


摘要

   On the basis of HPLC combined with UV and MS detection, all three targeted soy isoflavone aglycones, daidzein, genistein and glycitein in hydrolyzed extracts were successfully quantified within 25 min with formononetin used as the internal standard.
