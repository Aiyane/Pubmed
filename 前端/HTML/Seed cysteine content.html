文献标题:Foliar and Seed Application of Amino Acids Affects the Antioxidant Metabolism of the Soybean Crop.

PMID:28377778

作者：Teixeira WF(1), Fagan EB(2), Soares LH(2), Umburanas RC(1), Reichardt K(3), Neto DD(1).
年份：2017 Mar 21


摘要

   In both experiments, the use of cysteine, only in seed treatment and in both seed treatment and foliar application increased the activity of the enzyme PAL and catalase.

   Also in both experiments, the use of phenylalanine increased the activity of the enzyme PAL when the application was carried out as foliar application or both in seed treatment and foliar application.

   In the field experiment, the application of glutamate led to an increase in the activity of the catalase and PAL enzymes for seed treatment and foliar application.
