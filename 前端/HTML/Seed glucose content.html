文献标题:Soybean GmbZIP123 gene enhances lipid content in the seeds of transgenic Arabidopsis plants.

PMID:23963672 [Indexed for MEDLINE]

作者：Song QX(1), Li QT, Liu YF, Zhang FX, Ma B, Zhang WK, Man WQ, Du WG, Wang GD, Chen SY, Zhang JS.
年份：2013 Nov


摘要

   Overexpression of GmbZIP123 enhanced lipid content in the seeds of transgenic Arabidopsis thaliana plants.

   Higher levels of glucose, fructose, and sucrose were also found in seeds of GmbZIP123 transgenic plants.

文献标题:RNAi-mediated silencing of the myo-inositol-1-phosphate synthase gene (GmMIPS1) in transgenic soybean inhibited seed development and reduced phytate content.

PMID:16395584 [Indexed for MEDLINE]

作者：Nunes AC(1), Vianna GR, Cuneo F, Amaya-Farfán J, de Capdeville G, Rech EL, Aragão FJ.
年份：2006 Jun


摘要

   We have observed an absence of seed development in lines in which the presence of GmMIPS1 transcripts was not detected.

   Our results demonstrated an important correlation between GmMIPS1 gene expression and seed development.
