文献标题:Functional characterization of GmBZL2 (AtBZR1 like gene) reveals the conserved BR signaling regulation in Glycine max.

PMID:27498784

作者：Zhang Y(1)(2), Zhang YJ(3), Yang BJ(4), Yu XX(2)(5), Wang D(5), Zu SH(3), Xue HW(4), Lin WH(3)(6).
年份：2016 Aug 8


摘要

   GmBZL2(P216L) (GmBZL2*) in Arabidopsis results in enhanced BR signaling; including increased seed number per silique.

   BR treatment promotes the accumulation, nuclear localization and dephosphorylation/phosphorylation ratio of GmBZL2, revealing that GmBZL2 activity is regulated conservatively by BR signaling.

   Our studies not only indicate the conserved regulatory mechanism of GmBZL2 and BR signaling pathway in soybean, but also suggest the potential application of GmBZL2 in soybean seed yield.
